package: loro::math
specification pow(v1, v2) -> r
Returns the value of the first argument raised to the power of the second argument.
input:type:description
v1:real:one argument.
v2:real:other argument.
output:type:description
r:real:v1 to the power v2.
Precondition
true
Postcondition
''v1 to the power v2.''
