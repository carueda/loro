Demo projects
To install any of these projects in your LoroEDI:
1) Choose option "Install..." (Ctrl-I);
2) Write this page address in section "external project";
3) Click the "Browse" button;
4) Click the link of the desired project;
5) Accept the dialog box to complete installation.
That's it!
